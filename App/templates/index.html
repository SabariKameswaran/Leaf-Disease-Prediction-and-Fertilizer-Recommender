  • The Plant leaf disease dataset is built to provide an important guarantee of generalization capability of the proposed model.
  • Furthermore, to solve the problem that diseased Plant leaf images are insufficient and prevent overfitting of the CNN-based model in the training process, natural diseased Plant images are processed to generate sufficient training images via data augmentation technology.
  • Pooling and deconvolution are utilized simultaneously to integrate context and fuse features of the feature pyramid at the back of the SSD, via which higher small diseased object detection performance can be realized.

A deep convolutional neural network is employed for the real-time detection of Plant leaf diseases.
